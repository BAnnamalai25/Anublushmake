Makeup lessons are entirely bespoke and taught on either a one-to-one basis or in groups of two people. You can book me, a bridal makeup artist and experienced makeup lesson tutor.

The content is completely customized to suit your makeup goals and experience. When you arrive, we’ll discuss which looks you wish to learn and your biggest struggles with makeup. We also look through your existing makeup kit and brushes (please have them with you, no matter how big or small!) to assess which products are suitable and what you might need.

As part of the lessons, we work out the most flattering colour selections for you so that next time you shop for makeup, you won’t need to guess anymore and will know exactly which colours to go for.

We then go step-by-step through the all-important makeup basics, addressing facial proportions and how to maximize natural beauty. We then start creating the most flattering makeup style, demonstrating one step at a time, explaining and showing you how to do each particular step and which brushes to use, and you then repeat them guidance.

Importantly, we’ll also show you how to correct mistakes such as wobbly eyeliner or blotchy eyeshadow blending. We’ll share many unique makeup techniques to help you create long-lasting and beauty-boosting looks.
